About Glamour Cosmetics

Your trusted source for premium beauty products

Glamour Cosmetics is a leading beauty retailer offering a wide range of high-quality cosmetics and skincare products. Founded in 2010, we've been dedicated to helping our customers look and feel their best.

Our mission is to provide premium beauty products that are not only effective but also safe and cruelty-free. We carefully curate our collection from the most trusted brands in the industry.

At Glamour Cosmetics, we believe that beauty should be accessible to everyone. That's why we offer competitive prices and frequent promotions to make luxury beauty products affordable.

Our Velvet Matte Lipstick offers intense color payoff with a comfortable, lightweight feel. The long-lasting formula glides on smoothly and stays put for hours without drying out your lips.

  • Long-lasting matte finish
  • Rich, highly pigmented color
  • Comfortable, non-drying formula
  • Available in 12 stunning shades
Quantity:

Our Velvet Matte Lipstick is formulated with nourishing ingredients like Vitamin E and jojoba oil to keep your lips hydrated while providing a bold, matte finish. The highly pigmented formula ensures rich color payoff with just one swipe.

Perfect for all-day wear, this lipstick is transfer-resistant and won't feather or bleed. The sleek, retractable design makes it easy to apply on the go.

Key Ingredients:

  • Isododecane
  • Cyclopentasiloxane
  • Dimethicone
  • Tocopheryl Acetate (Vitamin E)
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil
  • Cera Alba (Beeswax)
  • May Contain: Iron Oxides (CI 77491, CI 77492, CI 77499), Titanium Dioxide (CI 77891), Red 7 Lake (CI 15850)
